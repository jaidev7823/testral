## STEP 3 — TEXT QC AUDIT (STRICT)

Audit **only the extracted visible text** for the following:

### 1. Typographical Errors
- Misspelled words  
- Incorrect word forms  
- Missing or extra characters  

### 2. Grammar Errors
- Sentence structure issues  
- Incorrect tense usage  
- Subject–verb disagreement  
- Incorrect or missing articles (`a / an / the`)  
- Broken or incomplete sentences  

### 3. Singular / Plural & Numbering Consistency
- Incorrect singular vs plural usage  
- Inconsistency within the same section or across related sections  
- Count-based mismatches (e.g., “1 items”, “Features” vs “Feature”)  

### 4. Capitalization Consistency
- Inconsistent casing within similar UI elements  
- Improper capitalization based on section context  
- Mixed styles without justification (e.g., “Login” vs “login”)  

### 5. Tone Consistency
- Text that breaks the established site tone  
- Language that feels out of place for the section or audience  
- Overly casual, overly formal, or mismatched wording **only if clearly inconsistent**
